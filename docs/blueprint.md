# **App Name**: EvaDevStudio Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Company Overview: Showcase a dynamic introduction to EvaDevStudio, highlighting expertise in mobile app, web development, and graphics design.
- Services Display: Detail service offerings like mobile app development, web development (Next.js + Firebase), and graphic design with modern descriptions.
- Project Portfolio: Exhibit a rich portfolio of projects including descriptions of ASTU Exam App, Store Management System, portfolio websites, graphic design projects, and mobile utility tools.
- Technology Showcase: List all technologies used (Next.js, Firebase, Flutter, Dart, TypeScript, etc.) in a visually engaging and informative manner.
- Founder Bio: Display a compelling biography of Samuel Tenkir, highlighting his experience and leadership style.
- Smart Contact Form: A modern contact form powered by an AI tool that ensures the form content is related to our services, redirecting generic inquiries to a FAQ.
- Firestore Integration: Collect user messages through contact form in the Firestore database.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A vibrant blue (#29ABE2) to represent technology and innovation. 
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5) for a clean, modern look.
- Accent color: A vivid orange (#FF8C00) to highlight key sections and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, to provide a modern, machined, objective, neutral look suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Modern, grid-based layout to ensure a clean, structured, and responsive design across all devices.
- Use a set of minimalist icons to represent services and technologies. Icons should be consistent and modern.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience and provide a sense of fluidity. Animations should be used sparingly to avoid distracting users.
